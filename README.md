# 🌍 Cost of Living Analyzer

## 💡 Project Idea
This project analyzes the cost of living across different cities and compares:
- Rent 🏠
- Food 🍔
- Transport 🚕
- Salary index 💰

The goal is to help people understand where they can afford to live.

---

## 📊 Dataset
Includes cities like:
- Amman
- Dubai
- Cairo
- Istanbul
- Berlin
- London

---

## 📈 Analysis
The project calculates:
- Total cost of living per city
- Salary vs cost ratio
- City ranking from cheapest to most expensive

---

## 🔥 Results
- Cheapest city identified
- Best city for salary value
- Comparison charts

---
## 🔎 Insights

- Dubai is the most expensive city in the dataset
- Cairo is the cheapest city
- Berlin offers a balanced cost-to-salary ratio
- Higher salary does not always mean better living conditions.
